IPHAS Data Release 2

Photometric survey of the Northern Galactic Plane

The INT Photometric H-Alpha Survey (IPHAS) offers a photometric catalogue of 200 million objects in the broad-band r'/i' and narrow-band H-alpha filters. It covers our Milky Way across galactic longitudes 29° to 215° and latitudes -5° to +5°, down to a depth of r'≈21 (Vega system.)

Key facts
  • Isaac Newton Telescope (2.5m)
  • Wide Field Camera
  • Filters: r', i', Hα
  • Aug 2003 - Nov 2012
  • 1.2" median seeing
  • Depth ≈ 21 (r'), 20 (i'), 20 (Hα)
